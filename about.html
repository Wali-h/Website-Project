Ferrari

History

Ferrari, founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 is a well known and popular italian luxury sports car manufacturer brand. The company started its production og cars in the late 1940s. The first vehicle with the name of the ferrari was the 125 S. Only 2 of these 2 seat sports/racing V12 cars were made.

F1

Ferrari is known for their pricey and performing cars. They perform and compete in many different events including F1. They mainly compete in F1 with their high performing vehicles. Ferrari has won the F1 a few times. Their current best driver is Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz, who have come 1st and 2nd at the 2022 Bahrain Grand Prix . In recent years they have not won many championships with their last one being won in 2007 with Kimi Raikkonen.

Modern Day Ferrari

To this day Ferrari has well known and popular cars such as the Ferrari Enzo and the Ferrari F40. The latest and most popular Ferrari is the Ferrari 296 GTB. It is a hybrid twin turbo V6, making it powerful but not much compared to other Ferraris. Although the engine may not be the most powerful it has a staggering 819 horsepower. The vehicle being from Ferrari the price point would not be low, which has a high price starting at $342 205.
